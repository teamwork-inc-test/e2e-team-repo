# Teamwork Inc Product Vision

**Date:** 2026-03-10
**Participants:** Tanya Owner (CEO), Tina Teammate (Head of Product)

## Context

Tanya and Tina aligned on the product vision for Teamwork Inc's collaboration platform.

Tanya: "We're building the anti-Slack. Instead of real-time noise, we want structured async decisions. Every conversation should produce a decision artifact that persists beyond the chat."

Tina: "The key insight is that decisions made in Slack disappear into scroll-back. We capture the decision at the moment it's made and link it to the context that produced it."

Key decisions:
- **Product category**: Structured async decision platform
- **Anti-pattern**: Real-time chat (Slack, Teams)
- **Core feature**: Decision capture at the point of consensus
- **Target market**: Remote teams with 10-50 people
- **Pricing**: $12/user/month
